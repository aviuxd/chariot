Customer Experience Managers

Chariot is a startup dedicated to solving your commute. By learning commuter habits and preferences, Chariot is building better routes, one neighborhood at a time. In order to collect and crunch the right data, Chariot is putting wheels on the street by launching a commuter shuttle between the Marina & Downtown during morning and evening rush hours. Service begins April.

This is an opportunity to be one of the first team members of a well-capitalized startup that has a long runway for growth and is solving a real pain point for thousands of people.

Every non-driver at Chariot starts as a Customer Experience Manager (including our Founder!) to learn how we’re solving a problem for our customers.

Initially, as a Customer Experience Manager you'll be in charge of:

  • Meeting & greeting customers
  • Accepting customer payments prior to boarding using your smartphone
  • Up-selling riders to roundtrip, weekly & monthly passes
  • Answering customer questions
  • Explaining Chariot's relative value vs. other transportation options

An awesome Customer Experience Manager should:

  • Have 1-3 years of customer/hospitality experience, preferred
  • Be outgoing, personable, friendly
  • Be reliable, dependable and on time
  • Excellent communication, interpersonal, organizational & listening skills
  • Enjoy face-to-face interaction
  • Be self-motivated to be Chariot's top weekly producer

Work schedule is flexible: part-time and full-time shifts are available. Chariot offers competitive compensation with weekly bonuses for production.

We expect to open an office later this year, and salaried positions will include: Community Manager, Design, HR & Recruiting, Finance, Head of Growth, and Corporate Sales.

To apply, please email Chariot a brief cover letter and resume to careers@chariotsf.com.

Chariot'eers (Experienced Drivers)

Chariot is a startup dedicated to solving your commute. By learning commuter habits and preferences, Chariot is building better routes, one neighborhood at a time. In order to collect and crunch the right data, Chariot is putting wheels on the street by launching a commuter shuttle between the Marina & Downtown during morning and evening rush hours. Service begins April.

This is an opportunity to be one of the first team members of a well-capitalized startup that has a long runway for growth and is solving a real pain point for thousands of people.

Every non-driver at Chariot starts as a Customer Experience Manager (including our Founder!) to learn how we’re solving a problem for our customers.

As a Chariot'eer, you'll be responsible for:

  • Safely driving a 15-passenger van
  • Routinely inspecting the van
  • Being a friendly and welcoming face for Chariot customers

An awesome Chariot’eer should:

  • Be outgoing, personable, friendly
  • Be reliable, dependable and on time
  • Have professional driving experience
  • Have a valid CA Class “C” driver’s license
  • Have a relatively clean driver’s record

Part-time and Full-time shifts are available.

Chariot offers very competitive compensation with opportunities for promotion and sponsored training for Class B licenses (bus/coach driving).

Top Chariot'eers will be considered for promotion to Team Captain within three months.

To apply, please email Chariot a brief cover letter and resume to careers@chariotsf.com.
