Solving your Commute

Chariot is now your best commuting option. Our service is frequent, comfortable and will save you money for happy hour and dry cleaning. Your Chariot launches in April between the Marina and Downtown.

Regular and Timely Service

Chariot’s "Chestnut Bullet" runs every 8 minutes from 6:30 to 9:30 AM. When it’s time to come home, know that Chariot will be there for you from 4:30 to 7:30PM.

Stress-Free Commuting

Chariot’s passenger vans provide a seat and comfortable ride from a number of centrally-located pickup and drop off spots on Chestnut Street and Downtown.

Sensible Value

Leave Uber for Saturday night. At $3.50 per ride, Chariot is as easy on your wallet as it is on your conscience. Your Chariot is available to book online or cash or card onboard.

Inbound service will run from 6:30AM with the last Chariot departing from the Marina Theater at 9:30AM.

Outbound service will start at 4:30PM with the last Chariot departing 2 Folsom at 7:30PM.

Chariots will depart every 8 minutes.

The fare is $3.50 each way. Registration for discounted monthly passes will begin soon!

While we’re currently focused on delivering incredible service to Marina commuters, Chariot looks forward to extending its service to other neighborhoods.
